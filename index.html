    Welcome

    Today's goal is to help you focus on what's truly important.

    Unfinished tasks don't carry over to the next day. Instead, you get to see a list of tasks you didn't finish the previous one.

    If any of them still matter, you'll need to re-add them from memory.

    By introducing intentional friction, Today encourages you to rethink priorities and focus on what matters.
